MusicXML Widget

Requirements: A MusicXML file to upload, and a notation viewer compatible with MusicXML (only tested in finale mind you!). Example before all options are ran. Example after.

What is this?

This widget lets you upload a MusicXML file, choose from a couple modifications, and finally download the resulting file.

Why?

My brother decided that for studying purposes it would be nice to see the scale degrees for a melody in a given lead sheet of jazz music. In order to make the music look nice, I had to remove the fingerings, rehearsal letters, and widen the staff spacing. Instead of doing that all in a magic "make it work" option, I thought it made just as much sense to separate them all out.
