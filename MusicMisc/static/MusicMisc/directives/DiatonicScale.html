What is this?

This widget lets you play around with different tonics and modes to see what the resulting diatonic scale looks like. I only made it because the functionality was required for the MusicXML Widget - and because I found it interesting.

A personal rambling

If you've ever learned to read music, you might know that all of the common modes will follow the same key signature flat/sharp pattern (BEADGCF and FCGDAEB) because their whole/half step patterns are all an offset of Ionion mode. So what happens when you re-order the whole/half step pattern? For example, click 'Add Custom Mode' and give this new mode the name Panda with the pattern '2121222' -> click [Submit]. Now in your list of modes choose 'Panda', choose a tonic of 'C' -> click [Submit]. Take a look at the resulting diatonic scale and you'll notice Panda mode doesn't follow the flat/sharp pattern (contains BEAG, no D). This is just one way to create some funky sounding scales when compared to the traditional Ionian/Aeolian sounds we're trained to hear. There are plenty more ways to tamper with the fundamentals in western music and create something blasphemous.
